Club Descriptions

In this club, we welcome everyone and anyone to stop by and play some games with fellow students in a fun and friendly environment. We cover all forms of games; from video games to board games and everything in between. Come on down and enjoy our selection of fun games to play, make some new friends, and take a break after a long day of classes!
