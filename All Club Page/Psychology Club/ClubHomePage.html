Club Descriptions

The club operates to provide an opportunity for students to learn about the professions of psychology and their varied practices. It helps advance students’ understanding of psychology’s role in our society and in their own, personal lives. For those interested in a career in psychology, the club provides opportunities for increased exposure to the professional roles and responsibilities of psychology professionals and to the education necessary for various careers in the field.
